Key Terms in Sustainability

Carbon Footprint
The total amount of greenhouse gases generated by our actions.
Upcycling
Reusing discarded objects or materials to create something of higher value.
Renewable Energy
Energy derived from natural sources that are constantly replenished like solar, wind, and hydro power.

Steps to Start a Sustainable Lifestyle

  1. Evaluate Your Current Habits
    1. Track energy and water usage
    2. Monitor plastic consumption
  2. Make Small Changes
    1. Switch to reusable bags
    2. Use public transportation
  3. Engage in Community
    1. Join local clean-up drives
    2. Support eco-friendly businesses
